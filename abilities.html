  • Influence Reactions Audience saves vs para. at -1 per 3 rawun lvls: shift reaction one category, rawun picks direction; opposite direction if target saves


  • Inspire Allies Rawun must spend 3 full rounds singing/reciting before battle, range 10 ft per rawun lvl, lasts 1 round per rawun lvl: +1 to hit or saves or +2 to allied morale


  • Counter Song Rawuns can counter magical attacks that involve songs, poetry, explanations, commands or suggestions but not verbal spells components or command words. Characters within 30 ft are immune as long as the rawun sings a counter song/recites a poem while taking no other action besides a slow walk. If the rawun is struck or fails a saving throw, the counter song ends. The rawun makes a save vs spell: success blocks the attack; on a failure the attack has its normal effects. The rawun can use this ability once per encounter.


  • Rawun Librarian If the Rawun owns and has access to a library worth at least 3,000 dinars and containing at least 100 books and/or scrolls, they get +1 to proficiency checks to research an item.


  • Evil Eye Once per 24 hours, the Rawun may make a Wisdom check. On a success they can inflict or remove the evil eye on another individual. On a failure to inflict, the Rawun suffers the evil eye; a failure to remove results in nothing happening.
